| Criteria | Details |
|---|---|
| Type | 1 Bedroom + Den/Office **or 2 Bedroom**, under $4,700 gross rent |

| Building | Highrise preferred |

## Key Questions to Ask Every Building
1. Do you have 1BR + den/office/flex floor plans? **(Skip if no)**
2. What is monthly parking pricing?
3. Are there any move-in specials or concessions?
4. Which floors have balconies?
5. Are walk-in closets available on 1BR units?
