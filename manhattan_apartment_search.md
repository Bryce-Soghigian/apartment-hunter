## Parking Strategy — Car + Motorcycle

### ⚠️ Important Context
Manhattan building parking is **separate** from rent and expensive. Budget **$400–$600/mo** for a car in Hell's Kitchen / Midtown. The motorcycle should go to a **dedicated moto-only facility** — most building garages won't want a track bike and conditions aren't ideal.

### Motorcycle / Track Bike Parking

Dedicated motorcycle-only facilities are far better than general garages for a track bike — proper security, no judgment, and actual workshop tools for pre/post track maintenance.

| Facility | Location | Price/mo | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ryders Alley — Midtown** ⭐ | W 55th St (bet. 9th & 10th Ave) | **$200/mo** | Best pick if living in Hell's Kitchen, Hudson Yards, or Midtown West. Heated clubhouse, battery tender, workbench, tire changer, air compressor. Background check required. |
| **Ryders Alley — Downtown** | 1 Ryders Alley (Financial District) | **$200/mo** | Same amenities, less convenient for Midtown |
| **Rising Wolf Garage** | 338 E 9th St (East Village) | **$200/mo** | Since 1997. 24hr keyed access, gear storage, outlets, air compressor |
| **Spacer — Chelsea** | Chelsea (near 10th Ave) | **~$188/mo** | Pickup/drop-off option; less community-focused |

> **Note:** NYC Municipal Parking Facilities explicitly exclude motorcycles. Don't assume a building garage will accept the bike.

## Key Questions to Ask Every Building
1. Do you have 1BR + den / home office / flex floor plans? **(Skip if no)**
2. What is monthly resident parking pricing for a car?
3. Is motorcycle parking available? (It's fine if not — Ryders Alley covers it)
4. Are there any move-in specials or concessions?
5. Walk-in closet on 1BR units?
6. Which floors have private balconies?
